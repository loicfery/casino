There are 3 reels containing 10 symbols in total, each reel contains :

- 1 seven

- 2 cherries

- 3 lemons

- 4 watermelons

The goal is to align 3 identical symbols, knowing that the winnings will be different for each combination.
The different wins are as follows:

- 3 sevens --> 160 tokens
- 3 cherries --> 25 tokens
- 3 lemons --> 8 tokens
- 3 watermelons --> 4 tokens
- 3 different symbols --> 0 tokens
